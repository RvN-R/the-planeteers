How to play The Planeteer Game

  1. Click on Play the Game and an item will be displayed.
  2. Click on the correct bin to dispose of the item.
  3. When the bin is selected, a popup window will open to display if answer is correct or incorrect.
  4. The popup wil also display fact information related to the item.
  • General Waste Bin:
    • Anything that cannot be recycled.
    • No electrical items.
    • No hazardous items.
  • Recycle Bin:
    • Paper.
    • Soft plastic.
    • Aluminium cans.
  • Glass Bin:
    • Clear glass jars and clear glass bottles, no lids or tops.
    • Green glass bottles, no corks or screw-tops.
    • Brown glass bottles, no corks or screw-tops.
  • Organic Waste Bin:
    • Food waste.
    • Garden waste.
