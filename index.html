Caring for our Planet

Planet Earth is the place that provides all the things that we need to live, but most importantly, we need to take care of it so it can keep giving us what we need to live here.

Caring for the Earth and the environment means that it will keep the planet healthy. Everyone can play their part by recycling items that can be recycled, upcycling items to give them a new re-purpose. Changing our mindset on how we use electricity, such as turning off lights when not needed and reduce the heating temperature a couple of degrees can help with energy consumption.

It's up to each and everyone of us on this planet to play our part. From us as individuals and families in our homes, to company's responsibity on how they conduct their business and that it is kind to our planet. There are plenty of ways to choose green and make choices to help our planet Earth and it starts from any age.

Making the right decisions to help conserve the Earth's natural resources such as air and water, so our generation and generations to come, will be able to enjoy our beautiful planet.

Did You Know?

We Make a Lot of Waste

In the UK we generate around 30 million tonnes of household rubbish in a year. That's the weight of almost 50,000 double decker buses!

We Buy Too Much Food

We throw away 4.5 million tonnes of edible food each year. This is responsible for the same CO2 emissions as flying from London to Australia many, many times!

We Use a Lot of Plastic

An estimated 13 billion plastic bottles are thrown away each year. If we lined them up they would stretch around the world about 32 times!

Plastic is Not Just Bad for the Oceans

The average person eats more than 18kg of plastic in their lifetime. Yuk!

How Can You Make a Difference?

Recycling Conserves Energy

We can light a 60W light bulb for up to 6 hours with the energy conserved from recycling one plastic bottle.

Recycling Saves Trees

17 trees are saved for every tonne of recycled paper.

Recycling Reduces Emissions

Recycling produces lower carbon emissions than making products from raw materials.

Recycling Saves Space

We have a limited space on the earth. Recycling keeps waste out of landfill leaving more room for open spaces.
